Biography

I am pursuing my PhD degree at Zhejiang University (ZJU). My supervisor is Prof. Xiaogang Jin. Before that, I received my B.E degree in 2009, from Zhejiang University of Technology. My research interests include computer complex system, autonomous driving, robotics and computer graphics.
